About Matt

Matt is a software developer from Akron, OH. He graduated from the University of Akron in 2022 with a degree in Computer Information Systems - Programming and also has a degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Stark State College in Ohio. Matt interned at RanchBookings.com where he worked in Django and Django REST Framework to make booking and management software for guest and cattle ranches. He did not get to ride any horses while working there. He and his wife moved to Dallas, TX, to be close to family and for better weather. They have 2 dogs, Boomer and Lucy.
